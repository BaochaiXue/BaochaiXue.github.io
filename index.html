About Me

I am enrolled in the Joint Bachelor’s Degree Program between Columbia University and the City University of Hong Kong. At Columbia I study Applied Mathematics and Computer Science, while at CityU I pursue the artificial intelligence stream with a minor in Mathematics. My coursework spans advanced programming, natural language processing, deep learning for computer vision and stochastic analysis.

My research centers on sample–efficient reinforcement learning and efficient deep learning for edge AI. At Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute I work on dataset filtering for in–context RL. Previously I built a reinforcement–learning scheduler for multi–model inference on edge GPUs at the Shenzhen Research Institute, CityU.

I lead a team in the International Collegiate Programming Contest and contribute to web development projects. For a complete overview of my background, publications and skills, please see my resume.
