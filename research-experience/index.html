Research Experience

  • Research Assistant, Shenzhen Research Institute, CityU (Aug 2023 – May 2024). Designed a reinforcement–learning scheduler to coordinate dual CUDA streams and multiple pruned CNN/Transformer variants (using DeGraph and torch–pruning) for real–time multi–model inference on edge GPUs, significantly improving resource utilization and latency.
  • Remote Predoc Researcher, Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (Mar 2025 – Jul 2025). Co–devised Learning History Filtering (LHF), a data–centric preprocessing scheme for in–context RL. Built a modular PyTorch pipeline and benchmarked LHF on Darkroom and Meta–World–ML1, achieving 12–46% average gains (up to 141% on Reach–Wall) with strong robustness to noisy or truncated histories.
